ACTIVITIES

1. GIRLS AND BOYS SCOUT

a. Personal Growth

There are also have a lot of extra curricular activities in AAMNHS. It has a good benefits if you want to join in their group. First, you will have a personal growth. It means that you will be productive ,creative ,and develop a positive sense of selff worth. You can also learn to make good decisions and resist peer pressure.

b. Life skills

Scouts learn practical skills like cooking, first aid, and money management. They also outdoor skills like survival and knot-tying.

c. Character Development

Scouts are learn values like respect for others, honesty ,and good conduct. They also learn to be trustworthy, loyal, helpful, and more.

d. Community

Scouts build close friendships and a sense of community with their troop. They also learn to serve others in their community.

e. Real World Skills

Scouts learn citizenships ,leadership, and outdoor ethics. They also earn badges through community service and other activities.

2. JOURNALIST

a. Interesting Works

Journalists report on a variety of topics, including current events, local developments, and political news. They may also have access to exclusive events, such as concerts, exhibitions, and rallies.

b. Travel

Journalists may travel to different places to cover stories, which can expose them to new perspectives and exciting situations.

c. Educating the Public

Journalists help educate the public about important matters, which can lead to better social and political decisions.

d. Applying concepts hands-on

Journalists have the opportunity to apply the concepts they learn in a variety of settings.

e. Working with Different People

Journalists work with different people in a variety of settings.

f. Freelance Opportunity

Freelance journalists can choose the type of article they want to write, where and when to do their writing, and the style of their articles.
